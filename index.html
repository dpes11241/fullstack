Our Vision

EL Education knows good schools. For thirty years, we’ve collaboratively designed some of the most successful schools in the nation. And after two years of chaos and struggle, public education is desperate for answers. More than ever before, the inequitable approaches school systems have relied on to get by are falling apart.

Our solutions are in wide demand nationally because after thirty years of research and development, we have a map for change. Our curriculum, developed by EL teachers with guidance from the top literacy experts in the nation, has been downloaded almost 15 million times since its release. The moment is now: transforming public education nationwide is within our grasp. Here’s our plan.
